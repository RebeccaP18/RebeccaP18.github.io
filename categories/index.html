Stuck in a music room.

I randomly remembered something last night as I lay awake in my bed. When I was in primary school, I distinctly recall deciding to spend one of my break times in the music room to practice piano. My music teacher at the time said that we were free to use the room during breaks so I took advantage of the opportunity. So when the bell rang for first break, I went straight to the music room and sat down at the piano.
